About Us



Do you need someone to talk to, are you afraid, do you need someone to listen to you, do you need help, do you want to report a rape case, we are here for you.

We are an Organisation that helps rape victims by listening to their story, counseling, directing on how to get medical attention, provide online outreach programme. Here, you are all welcome because no one judges you.
